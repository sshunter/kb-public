# Teriyaki Chicken

Servings: TBD
Preparation Time: TBD

## Overall

### Ingredients

- noodles, 8 ounces (SAM: will probably do rice)
- boneless chicken thighs (can use 1lb of any protein you like)
- veggies of choice (e.g. brocolli, carrots, onions) , 2 1/2 cups
- teriyaki sauce (see below)

### Cooking Instructions (SAM wrote these)

0. OPTIONAL: Marinate chicken with sauce (see below) if time allows.
1. Cook the chicken thru. Slice into strips and set aside.
2. Steam the veggies if frozen and then maybe sear them in a pan.
3. Prepare the sauce.
4. Cook the noodles as appropriate
5. Serve on a plate and garnish if we are getting fancy.

## Sauce, Version 1

### Ingredients (v1)

- soy sauce, 1/2 cup
- honey (or maple syrup or brown sugar), 1/4 cup
- rice vinegar, 2 tablespoons
- minced garlic, 1 teaspoon
- minced ginger, 1 teaspoon
- corn starch, 2 teaspoons
- water, equal parts to corn starch
- sesame oil, 1 tablespoon

### Cooking Instructions (v1)

1. Add soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, garlic, and ginger to pot.
2. Bring to medium boil
3. Reduce heat and simmer for about 5 minutes
4. Mix corn starch with equal parts water, then whisk into simmering sauce.
   Simmer for 1-2 more minutes.

## Sauce, Version 2 (More Authentic)

### Ingredients (v2)

- soy sauce, 1/4 cup
- mirin (rice cooking wine), 1/4 cup\
- sugar, 2 tablespoons
- OPTIONAL: sake, 1 tablespoon
- grated ginger, 2 teaspoons
- sesame oil, 1 tablespoon
- corn starch, 2 teaspoons
- water: equal parts to corn starch
- OPTIONAL: sesame seeds (SAM: Toasted?) and green onions for garnish

#### Doubled

- soy sauce, 1/2 cup
- mirin (rice cooking wine), 1/2 cup\
- sugar, 1/4 cup
- OPTIONAL: sake, 2 tablespoon
- grated ginger, 4 teaspoons (1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon)
- sesame oil, 2 tablespoon
- corn starch, 4 teaspoons
- water: equal parts to corn starch
- OPTIONAL: sesame seeds (SAM: Toasted?) and green onions for garnish


### Cooking Instructions (v2)

1. Add soy sauce, mirin, sugar, ginger, and sesame oil
2. Bring to medium boil
3. Reduce heat and simmer for about 5 minutes
4. Mix corn starch with equal parts water, then whisk into simmering sauce.
   Simmer for 1-2 more minutes.
5. OPTIONAL: Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
