# No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars

## Ingredients

### Base

- Peanut Butter (1 cup)
- Honey or Maple Syrup (1/4 cup)
- Oat flour (1/4 cup), you can make something close enough by blending oat-meal
  in food processor.
- Salt (pinch, optional)

### Topping

- Peanut Butter (1/4 cup)
- Dark Chocolate (1 cup)
  
## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients for base until smooth
2. Pour into lined 8x8ish inch baking dish and smooth/level it out
3. Set aside base
4. Mix ingredients for topping in microwave safe bowl.
5. Microwave in 30 second intervals, stirring after each interval until melted
6. Combine
7. Pour topping over base in dish and smooth/level it out
8. Freeze for 1 hour to set
9. Cut into squares or bars

Store in refrigerator or freeze
